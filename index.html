Brief Biography

Dr. Changyi Ma joined the School of Artificial Intelligence at Jilin University (JLU) in 2025 as a tenure-track Associate Professor. She received her Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK-Shenzhen) (QS #36, 2025), advised by Prof. Shuguang Cui (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Wenye Li. She had received the Presidential Award for Outstanding Doctoral Student.

Her research interests include similarity matrix calibration, noisy label learning, continual learning, and large language models (LLMs).

I am always looking for self-motivated PhD/RA/Visiting/Master students. Please send me an email to query the detail: changyima@jlu.edu.cn.
